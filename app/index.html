KINIMA

Kinima would like to invite you to the superb yet forgotten world of cine-variety shows. What started in the music halls during the 1850’s - with nightly bills of singers, dancers, and specialty acts, - quickly grew into something new and exciting with the advent of cinema.

During the early part of the 20th century, and on the cusp of a new scientific age, the entertainment style of the music halls now known as ‘variety’ was incorporated by some of the more forward thinking cinemas of the time to create a new and exciting experience; a mix of live performance and a moving picture.

Our aim is to bring this lost art to you by invariably providing a fresh selection of fast-paced programmes supporting a genre defining film based in the spectacular Cinema Museum. Giving you, the viewer, a space to relax, unwind, socialise with friends, family, and other film lovers.

The catalyst for this will be the location itself, London’s Cinema Museum; a mind boggling collection of memorabilia, equipment, and oddities that showcase the magnificence and relevance of the cinema experience.

We will have musicians, dancers, and performers of all types delivering entertaining short acts to magnetize you into a world of pure merriment. We will take you to new places by revisiting the past, all the while having an indelible time.

Join us as we break the fourth wall and enter Kinima.
